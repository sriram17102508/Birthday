Brief History of Dr. Somu Chinnusamy

Dr. Somu Chinnusamy is an entrepreneur, educator, and technology enthusiast from Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. He is the Chairman & Managing Director and one of the founders of ECLearnix EdTech Private Limited, an organization focused on transforming education through technology, research support, international conferences, training programs, and digital innovation.

Along with co-founders Dr. Pravin Thangavelu and Saravana Kumar Madheswaran, he established ECLearnix with the vision of bridging the gap between academia, research, and industry. The company helps students, researchers, faculty members, and institutions through international conferences, journal publications, AI-focused training, UX/UI design, web development, and innovation programs.

Under his leadership, ECLearnix has built a growing academic ecosystem connecting thousands of faculty members and colleges through conferences, research collaborations, and skill-development initiatives. The organization aims to make quality education, research publication, and global networking more accessible through technology-driven solutions.

Dr. Somu's vision is centered on empowering learners, researchers, and professionals with AI-driven education, innovation, and career opportunities while creating a globally connected learning community.
